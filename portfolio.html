Week Eight

Our seventh task was to produce two charts supporting a refuting an argument made at the Festival of Economics

‘Lockdown increased the appeal of crypto - there was a lack of sports betting, people were looking for a rush - this lead to the gamification of crypto’

While we cannot establish causality from this data, limited sports betting lead to people seeking similar thrills elsewhere. It is plausible that more people looking for a rush entered the crypto scene.
